About MessianicChurchFinder

Our Mission

MessianicChurchFinder was established to connect believers with Sabbath-keeping churches across Kenya. Our platform serves as a directory for Messianic congregations that observe the biblical Sabbath from Friday sunset to Saturday sunset.

We aim to make it easy for people to find churches that align with their faith practices and to support the growth of these congregations across the country.

The Sabbath

The Sabbath is a holy day of rest commanded by Elohim in the Ten Commandments (Exodus 20:8-11). It begins at sunset on Friday and ends at sunset on Saturday, following the biblical reckoning of days (Genesis 1:5, "There was evening, and there was morningâ€”the first day").

Sabbath-keeping churches maintain this practice as an essential part of worship and obedience to Elohim's commandments.

The Movement in Kenya

The Zionist and Messianic church movement in Kenya has grown significantly with thousands of members across the country. These churches emphasize the word of Elohim (God), observing the biblical feasts and dietary laws alongside their faith in Yahshuah (Jesus) the Messiah.
